# Physics Simulations

A collection of various physics simulation projects that cover topics ranging from classical mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and quantum mechanics.
I am wanting to build upon my knowledge of physics I learned during my undergraduate degree, as well as improving my various programming skills.

	---
	
	## Features
	- Projectile Motion with Drag/Air Resistance. (completed)
	- Harmonic Oscillators.
	- Lorenz System.
	- Monte Carlo Methods.
	- Double Penduulum/Chaotic Systems.
	- N-Body Gravitational Simulations.
	- Heat Transfer Simulations.
	- Wave Propagation and Schrodinger Equation Simulation.
	- Rigid Body Dynamics (Gyroscope, Rotating Bodies).
	- Collision Simulations (Elastic/Inelastic, Ideal Gas Law).
	- Control Systems (PID Controller).
	- 6DOF Simulations (Projectile Systems).
